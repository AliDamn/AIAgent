## Business Requirements Document (BRD)
### 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the business requirements for improving the Know Your Customer (KYC) processes. The primary objective is to automate and streamline the KYC framework, thereby reducing onboarding times and enhancing compliance.

### 2. Goal and Background
The objective of this initiative is to transition from a manual KYC process characterized by inefficiencies to a streamlined, digital-first approach that enhances compliance and customer satisfaction.

### 3. Detailed Business Problem Description
Current KYC processes are cumbersome, with issues such as long onboarding times, high operational costs, and compliance challenges.

### 4. Business Objectives (SMART Format)
- Specific: Automate the KYC process for digital submissions.
- Measurable: Reduce customer onboarding time from 48 hours to 2 hours.
- Achievable: Implement a phased rollout of automation tools.
- Relevant: Aligns with regulatory compliance and operational efficiency goals.
- Time-bound: Complete the transition by the end of Q4 2024.

### 5. Functional Requirements
1. The system shall allow customers to submit identification documents through a secure online portal.
2. The system shall automatically verify submitted documents against government databases.
3. The system shall conduct real-time background checks against watchlists.

### 6. Non-Functional Requirements
- Performance: The system should support at least 500 simultaneous user sessions.
- Security: Compliance with ISO/IEC 27001 for information security.

### 7. Business Rules
1. Customers must be verified within regulatory timeframes.
2. All customer data must be encrypted during transmission and at rest.

### 10. KPIs and Success Metrics
- Processing Time: Reduction to 2 hours per KYC process.
- Compliance Rate: Achieve a compliance adherence rate of 100%.

## Requirements / Use Cases
### Use Case 1: Document Submission
- **Use Case ID**: UC-001
- **Actors**: Customer, System Administrator
- **Preconditions**: Customer must be logged in.
- **Main Flow**:
  - Customer uploads identification documents.
  - System validates and confirms receipt.
- **Postconditions**: Submitted documents are stored for verification.

### Use Case 2: Document Verification
- **Use Case ID**: UC-002
- **Actors**: System, Compliance Officer
- **Preconditions**: Documents must have been submitted.
- **Main Flow**:
  - System verifies documents against government databases.
  - Compliance officer reviews the verification report.

### Additional Use Cases Matched to User Stories
Each functional requirement corresponds to specific use cases and user stories, detailing actions, potential errors, and rules.
